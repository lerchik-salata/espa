Participant Journey

Registration

  • Agree to Terms & Conditions and apply
  • Go through a round of interviews
  • Get accepted into the program

Bootcamp

1 week

Las Vegas, NV

  • 3 days of intense and fun learning with hands-on sessions and on-site experts
  • After-hours social events for the group
  • Electronic presentation material for participants to keep and study
  • Access to FIL lease to familiarize themselves with the Filecoin mainnet

Internship

3 Months

Remote

  • Optional online sessions with dedicated staff for one-on-one review and collaborative insights
  • Access to hours of self-guided Educational modules per week
  • Monthly online group sessions and roundtable discussions
  • ESPA will be simultaneously sealing up to 1PiB Raw Capacity

Residency

6-9 Months

Remote

  • Go-to-market independently & participate in broader Filecoin ecosystem
  • Utilize Lotus online deals and Layer 2 tools like Estuary, CIDgravity, BidBot
  • Brainstorm and prepare business plan to expand Web3 Storage Providing capabilities
  • Access ESPA Fellows for consulting (dedicated ESPA community Slack)
  • Access ESPA Fellows for consulting (dedicated ESPA community Slack)

Graduation

6-9 Months

Remote

  • Webcast presentation of redacted business plan and “Demo Day”
  • Storage system exit strategy: data transfer or surrender of system
  • Access to ESPA “investor friends of the program”
  • Certification upon completion and evidence of proficiency
  • Participant to decide on next steps:
    • Extend operation and work use as a demonstration of business
    • Transfer FIL to own operation after payoff of FIL collateral lease
    • Exit program after payoff of FIL collateral lease
